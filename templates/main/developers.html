TOP-BENTLEY DESIGN

Great collaboration of Top South Florida-based Property Developer Dezer Development, luxury automaker Bentley Motors, incomparable architectural firm Sieger Suarez Architects is the opportunity to create unique innovations in construction of Bentley Residences Miami project.

The first Bentley - branded residential tower in the world, one of the largest residential condo towers of the coastline of the South Florida will contain more than 200 apartments and stand at 749ft and more than 60 stories high.

Among the most notable amazing design solutions of the tower are its unusual cylindrical form and floor-to-ceiling windows; picturesque ocean and bay views in every apartment; Bentley-patented car elevator and in-unit multi-car garage; huge area of private balconies with pools, saunas and outdoor showers; environmentally safe building materials; eco-energy consumption.
