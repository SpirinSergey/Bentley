Inside Amenities

Each of the two hundred luxury apartments, developed with the participation of Bentley specialists, is promised to be equipped with panoramic windows, a private balcony with a swimming pool, an outdoor shower and a sauna.

Moreover, the residents of the luxury tower are able to get into their apartments without leaving their cars. A private garage, available for each apartment, is located on the same floor.

A lounge area with a life-size sculpture of a Bentley car will be equipped in the lobby of the skyscraper.
