about

New heights are conquered by the British automobile company Bentley, one of luxury car manufacturers involved in residential projects in Miami as well as Porsche, Aston Martin.

Fascinating views of endless sunny beaches of Miami coastline, turquoise waters of the Atlantic Ocean, opulent resort-style living, luxury boutiques and souvenir shops, parks for active sports, spa, clubs, theatres, cafes and restaurants - all these natural and residential features of Sunny Isles Beach will become a permanent part of life in the Bentley Residences.

Exclusivity of Bentley Tower

Exclusivity of the tallest building in the South Florida, resembling an exterior of Bentley Continental, is felt not only in its incredible height, shape and color, but in huge living areas filled with ocean air, large balconies and terraces with individual spa facilities, elevators for personal cars and many other technological innovations that make our life carefree and luxurious.

Groundbreaking technologies and ecological approaches to the construction of Bently's condo tower, decoration of apartments, electricity consumption and minimization the impact of the development on local wildlife deserve every delight!
